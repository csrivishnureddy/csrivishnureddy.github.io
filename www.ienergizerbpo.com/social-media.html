Social Media

Social Media

Social and Content Moderation

With millions of people actively using social media platforms on a daily basis, a multi-channel strategy, effective monitoring process and round the clock customer support are imperative to maintaining and safeguarding the online reputation of your brand. All comments about your brand online, regardless of their tone, are there to stay, so an effective content moderation strategy can keep your brand strong and safe.

The actual need of content moderation is at social media platforms where a thriving community of fans and influencers are free to post anything per their state of mind through online conversations with community and by uploading content in form of comments, reviews, blogs, photos etc.

At iEnergizer, we offer real-time comment moderation services, image moderation services, live streaming video moderation and audio moderation that covers social networks, forums, chatrooms, games, proprietary customer channels and more. Our teams of moderators identify and remove offensive content, and keep your website, social media channels and communities safe and positive. We are a premier resource for social and user-generated content moderation with hundreds of Moderators onboard.

Social Media Monitoring

Our Mods monitor, moderate and manage official and brand-specific social media channels, gauge customer sentiment and help organize community engagement campaigns. Your brand will be safe and thrive in our hands.

User Generated Content Moderation

Our Mods help with text moderation for forums and filter user generated images, audio and video contributions - We will help you catch sensitive posts before they ever reach your community.
