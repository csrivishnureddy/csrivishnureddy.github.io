iEnergizer COO Adarsh Kumar Honored with Leadership Award at Times Business Awards 2025

We are proud to announce that Mr. Adarsh Kumar, Co-founder and Chief Operating Officer of iEnergizer, has been awarded the Leadership Award for Excellence in Global Outsourcing Solutions at the Times Business Awards 2025, held on Friday.

The prestigious event, hosted in the presence of acclaimed actress Sonali Bendre, recognized distinguished leaders and organizations across industries who have demonstrated outstanding achievements, innovation, and impact. The Times Business Awards continue to serve as a benchmark of excellence, celebrating the visionaries who are shaping the future of Indian and global business.

Adarsh Kumar’s recognition is a significant milestone, reflecting iEnergizer’s consistent focus on operational excellence, innovation in service delivery, and its growing global footprint in the outsourcing domain. With over two decades of leadership experience, Adarsh Kumar has played a pivotal role in driving iEnergizer’s transformation into one of the leading integrated business process outsourcing companies.

Upon receiving the award, Mr. Kumar remarked:

“This recognition is a reflection of the dedication, innovation, and relentless pursuit of excellence by the entire iEnergizer family. Global outsourcing isn’t just about scale — it’s about people, partnerships, and purpose. I’m deeply humbled and incredibly proud of what we’ve built together.”

Under his leadership, iEnergizer continues to deliver cutting-edge, customer-centric solutions to clients across the globe, reinforcing its reputation as a trusted partner in business process transformation.

We congratulate Mr. Adarsh Kumar on this well-earned recognition and look forward to continuing our journey of excellence and impact under his strategic direction.

CIO POWER LIST 2024 Recognizes Kapil Pal as the IT, ITES & BPO ICON

CIO Power List is a list of THE most INFLUENTIAL CIOs and Digital Leaders in India. By harnessing technological advancements and their influence over growing businesses, these CIOs are leading the disruption wave, redefining engagement strategies, and leveraging opportunities to enhance business growth.

CIO POWER LIST winners are finalized on the basis of an algorithm that takes into account a wide range of data collected across multiple channels making the entire process unique. The 360-degree selection process is based on several criteria and multiple parameters in the Business and Technology Categories.

This year was the 10th edition of the CIO Power List recognition. Over the last 10 years, this platform has fostered a dynamic community of CIOs, evolving into a powerful and prestigious platform of recognition, and has become a benchmark in the industry.

This esteemed recognition was presented at a grand felicitation ceremony which brought together over 200+ CIOs and Digital Leaders from across the country to celebrate the achievements of India's most distinguished CIOs and Digital Leaders. This year, the CIO Power List honoured 111 CIOs, recognizing them as Business Icons and Technology Icons for their outstanding contribution and leadership in the industry.

Anoop Mathur, Founder, CORE Media, said, “Congratulations Kapil for featuring in the CIO POWER LIST 2024 as Business Icon. This recognition is a testament to your visionary leadership as a CIO and Digital Leader. Amid the dynamic digital landscape and evolving customer trends, where technology is the driving force behind every enterprise, the CIO Power List honour compliments your role as the strategic architect of digital value, driving business transformation and innovation for business growth.”

About CORE Media:

Founded in 2012, CORE Media (Centre of Recognition & Excellence) is a multi-platform new age niche media company that owns and operates proprietary marketing events, account-based marketing (ABM) solutions, and digital platforms with a focus on ICT Enterprises, the CIO Community, the CFO Community and Technology Startups. Its programs have a combined reach of around 56,000 ICT decision-makers and influencers across all key sectors.

iEnergizer and CTO KAPIL PAL received “The Resilient 100” award at the prestigious CSO100 Awards and Symposium! For Exemplary Cyber and Information Security Measures


We are thrilled to announce that Kapil Pal, our Chief Technology Officer at iEnergizer, has been honoured with "The Resilient 100" award at the prestigious CSO100 Awards and Symposium!

The sixth annual edition of the CSO100 Awards and Symposium is an exclusive, by-invite ‘in-person’ event attended by leading IT decision-makers from enterprises across India. These professionals have demonstrated the highest standards of competence in cyber and information security, showcasing initiatives that deliver outstanding business value amidst a constantly evolving digital and threat landscape.

The Resilient 100: This accolade recognizes one hundred award-winning IT leaders and CSOs who have driven positive business outcomes with effective risk mitigation strategies. These individuals have proven to be innovators, leaders, and precise executors over the past year, making significant impacts in their respective fields.

We are incredibly proud of Kapil Pal for being recognized among the top cybersecurity leaders of 2023. His dedication to innovation and resilience in the face of evolving challenges has been instrumental in driving iEnergizer’s success.

"Congratulations to Kapil Pal for receiving 'The Resilient 100' award at the CSO100 Awards and Symposium. This recognition is a testament to your outstanding leadership, innovative spirit, and unwavering commitment to cybersecurity excellence. Your efforts have significantly contributed to our success and continue to set new benchmarks in the industry. We are proud to have you on our team!" says Adarsh Kumar, Co-founder & COO of iEnergizer.
