Sachin Vij

Telecom Sales and Support

Sachin has been associated with iEnergizer since 2003 & holds expertise in the Telecom Sales, Service and Support domains. He has been versatile in accommodating clients located across the globe from North America, UK and Pan India, this enables him to be more strategic, demographically. He has a vast knowledge of driving inbound, outbound and hybrid Telecom processes (Inbound Customer service, Technical Help desk, Welcome Calling, Service queries, Upselling, etc). His core competency is in data analytics and Strategy Planning helps in enhancing customer journey with the network provider.

Sachin holds a bachelor’s degree in Commerce from the University of Delhi. He is equally a passionate cricketer and loves to both watch and play cricket.

Rajiv Batra

On-shore E-Commerce

Rajiv has been affiliated with IEnergizer for over 15 years, involved with E-commerce businesses. Holding a master’s degree in Operation Management, he has been an integral part of various electronic commerce platforms, playing a vital role in manifesting growth of these businesses. In today’s globally dynamic and diverse environment, e-commerce is a shapeshifter whose mission is various and evolving. With immense operational expertise, he has assisted in collaborating with several e-commerce business partners and played a vital role in contributing to their overall business growth, concentrating on both operational and strategic oriented approach.

Given the stratospheric rise in the e-commerce industry in recent years, he has collaborated with different functions within the organization to accommodate functionality and growth of the several businesses he is associated with.

Jaya Pillai

Vice President

Jaya leads the Investor and Shareowner services domain at iEnergizer. A seasoned business leader with a 16 year proven track record of execution at both the tactical and strategic levels, she has been with iEnergizer for over 13 years managing highly regulated financial transaction processing work like corporate actions, employee stock purchase plan, asset management, banking and hedge funds. A key member of the integration team posts major acquisitions, she has a strong background of spearheading remote and onsite transitions of complex and niche transactions; project governance with rigorous risk and control protocols, providing her with a fundamental understanding of our equity businesses combined with broad expertise in the customer experience field, growing customer relationships and enhancing customer loyalty.

Jaya holds a bachelor’s degree from North Gujarat University with a focus in Economics and English. She is also a certified Green Belt Lean Six Sigma professional.
